1. Art54

At least one of the claims appears to lack novelty in the sense of Articles 54(1) and (2) EPC because all its features are known from the prior art:

1. Art 54

D1 discloses A splice holder assembly for receiving optical fibers to be housed in units in protective tubes, the splice holder assembly comprising: a splice tray having a bottom and sides defining an open-topped enclosure: the splice tray having at least one splice organizing module mounted to the bottom, and the splice tray having retainers extending from the inner surfaces of the sides of the splice tray at a level above the bottom; a card to be fitted into the splice tray and to be held in position by the retainers; and at least one splice cassette to be fitted in the splice tray and supported by the card: the splice cassette uncluding a bottom portion in which is mounted at least one splice organizing module, and a cover adapted to fit over the bottom portion to enclose the splice cassette.
